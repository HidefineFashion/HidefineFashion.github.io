ABOUT US

We are a renowned manufacturer and exporter of finished leather garments, footwear, and accessories based in India. Our products are distributed to various countries around the world.

Stitching Together Style, Sustainability and Technology

Our reputation is built on our expertise in using fine hides to create a diverse range of outerwear, footwear, and accessories. We are committed to ethical and sustainable practices throughout our manufacturing process.

We strive to establish lasting partnerships by emphasizing complete transparency and collaboration at every stage of production
